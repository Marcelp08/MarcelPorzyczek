Web Developer (Frontend)

Job Description: We are seeking a talented Frontend Web Developer to join our dynamic team. This role involves developing responsive layouts, collaborating with design and backend teams, ensuring cross-browser compatibility, and maintaining company websites.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop responsive web layouts using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Ensure compatibility across devices and browsers.
  • Collaborate with designers to bring mockups to life.
  • Maintain and improve existing websites.

Qualifications:

  • Proficient in HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript.
  • Experience with responsive design and Flexbox/Grid.
  • Familiarity with version control (e.g., Git).
  • Attention to detail and strong problem-solving skills.

Responsibilities:

Qualifications:

  • Strong problem-solving and debugging skills.
